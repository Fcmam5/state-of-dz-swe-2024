Better Internet & QoS‚Äã

The Ministry of Post and Telecommunications led by Mr. Karim BIBI TRIKI is taking big steps in improving the Algerian ICT scene and infrastructure.

To improve the quality of service, the Regulatory Authority of Post and Electronic Communications (ARPCE) launched Jawdati, an application that measures the quality of Internet service in Algeria. Algeria Telecom on its end is very active on its social media and follow up on queries and complaints from customers.

Recently, the ministry announced that Algerian equipped capacity of the country's network jumped from 7.8 to 9.8 between 2022 and early 2024 to meet the increasing demand for high speed internet.
